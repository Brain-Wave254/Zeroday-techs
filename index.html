The Challenge Identified


A project to investigate and improve services in ModiAdvocates law firm

Modiadvocates Law Firm is facing challenges in efficiently managing client appointments and consultations. The current manual
booking process is time-consuming, prone to errors, and lacks the ability to provide clients with real-time availability for appointments.
This results in missed opportunities, scheduling conflicts, and a suboptimal client experience.

Our solution is to integrate a comprehensive booking system directly into Modiadvocates Law Firm's website. This booking system will
allow clients to schedule appointments with lawyers easily and conveniently, eliminating the need for manual booking processes.
The system will provide real-time availability of appointments, streamline the scheduling process, and enhance overall efficiency for
both the firm and its clients.
